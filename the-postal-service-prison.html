This Place Is A Prison (Song by The Postal Service)

I was inspired by the lyrics of the song This Place Is A Prison by The Postal Service and I since I love playing with extreme angles, the inspiration translated to a prison complex from a bird's eye view. Sharpie on paper, digitalized and colored in Figma, a light gradient was added to create some additional perspective and depth in the bricks and pavement.

No, unfortunately, The Postal Service band is not affiliated to this project. :shrug:
